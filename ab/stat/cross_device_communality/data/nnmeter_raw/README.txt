Place the nn-Meter *.jsonl prediction-latency files here to regenerate the
external-replication results. Download from the official nn-Meter dataset
release (datasets.zip, ~114 MB) at https://github.com/microsoft/nn-Meter and
unzip the per-search-space *.jsonl files into this directory. These raw files
(~1.8 GB) are NOT bundled here because of size. The derived CSVs already in
../nnmeter/ let you inspect every reported number without this download.
